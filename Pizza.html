Description:

Pizza is a delicious dish which can have a large variety of toppings, but in this recipe we are going to focus mainly on Buffalo Chicken Pizza.

Ingredients:

  • 1 homemade pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) ranch dressing
  • 1.5 cups (200g) shredded chicken breast
  • 1/3 cup (33g) thinly sliced red onion, divided
  • 1.5 cups (170g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2Tablespoons ranch dressing
  • 2Tablespoons buffalo sauce
  • 2Tablespoons sliced green onions

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Brush the pan with olive oil.
  4. Transfer the dough to the oiled pan.Flatten the dough so it almost covers the entire pan.
  5. Spread the ranch dressing evenly over the dough.
  6. Top with the shredded chicken breast and red onion.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and top with mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Drizzle with buffalo sauce and top with sliced green onions.
  11. Slice and serve hot.
